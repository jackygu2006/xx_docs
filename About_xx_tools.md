* Generate multiple accounts off-line, just one click.

![](https://tva1.sinaimg.cn/large/008i3skNgy1gwfp6fxse5j30sq1ewq5q.jpg)

![](https://tva1.sinaimg.cn/large/008i3skNgy1gwfp5r3ajsj30re1iawhn.jpg)
